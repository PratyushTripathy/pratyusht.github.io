Contact me!

I am always happy and willing to get in touch with people with overlapping research interests. My primary research interest is developing methods to understand socio-economic characteristics of population in the developing world using spatial data. In addition, I actively work on a broad range of urban remote sensing topics including but not limited to intra-urban heterogeneity, urban neighborhoods, finer-resolution population distribution, 3D city models, and urban growth models, etc. Furthermore, I am a big fan and intermittent contributor to the open-source community.
